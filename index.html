5 Healthy Breakfast receipes for Software Engineers

Oatmeal

  • Take 100g of Oats pour in container
  • Add 150 ml of water.
  • Add 100 ml of milk.
  • For tasty purposes Add pumpkin seeds and Almonds.
  • Keep it in Fridge overnight.
  • Hola! Tasty oat meal is ready in the morning
  • Time required to prepare 60 seconds!

Sprouts Salad

  • Take 20g of Moong beans,20g of Kidney beans,20g chick peas soak it in a jar containing 2 glass of water
  • Remove water after 8 hours.
  • Now take a cloth cover seeds and gently spray water
  • leave it overnight in a dark area
  • Check in morning seeds germinates
  • Now boil sprouts in water for 7 min at high flame to kill bacteria.
  • Add cucumber choped pieces,tomatos chopped pieces,chat masala,Lemon juice as per your taste.
